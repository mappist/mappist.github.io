Welcome!

My name is Caleb Winebrenner.

In the spring of 2020, I began a Master's Program in GIS, after nearly ten years of working in the humanities. As I laid the groundwork for my new career as a cartographer, geographer, and analyst, I knew that I didn't want to completely abandon the humanities, either.

So, in the early spring of 2021, I began to put together a portfolio. I wanted an opportunity to share some of my essays from my previous career -- all every centered on place, community, ecology, and connection to the land -- as well as the maps and reports I am making now. The truth is, no accurate portfolio of my work could lack either part.

This is still a work in progress. Eventually, you will find my CV and links to different project pages. In the meantime, feel free to email me: winebren@usc.edu
